Duration


The duration is a combinaison of two parts. The first one is ued to describe the normalized and formatted expression of the duration, and the second one is a textual and not formatted description, if it can not be written in an another way, or if the textual information is required to complete the formatted value.

The duration must be written in the same way as this example : « P2Y1M2DT1H20M25.55S ». In this example, « P » is used to show that the string which follow that letter is an expression of a duration; « 2Y » = 2 years; « 1M » = 1 months; « 2D » = 2 days; « T » indicates that the string which follow is using the temporal représentation « hour, minute, second »; « 1H » = 1 hour; « 20M » = 20 minutes; « 25.55S » = 25,55 seconds
